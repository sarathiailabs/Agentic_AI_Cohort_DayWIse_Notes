# 📜 Day 6 – Resources: Prompt Engineering

Here is a curated list of official documentation, research papers, tools, and courses to deepen your Prompt Engineering skills.

---

## 📖 Official Documentation & Guides
1.  **OpenAI Prompt Engineering Guide:**  
    [https://platform.openai.com/docs/guides/prompt-engineering](https://platform.openai.com/docs/guides/prompt-engineering)  
    *The gold standard guide mapping out 6 key tactics for prompt design.*
2.  **Anthropic Prompt Engineering Interactive Tutorial:**  
    [https://docs.anthropic.com/en/docs/build-with-claude/prompt-engineering](https://docs.anthropic.com/en/docs/build-with-claude/prompt-engineering)  
    *An interactive, developer-focused course on writing prompts for Claude.*
3.  **Google Gemini Prompting Guide:**  
    [https://ai.google.dev/gemini-api/docs/prompting](https://ai.google.dev/gemini-api/docs/prompting)  
    *Best practices and patterns optimized for the Gemini model family.*

---

## 🔬 Key Research Papers
1.  **Chain-of-Thought Prompting Elicits Reasoning in Large Language Models (Wei et al.):**  
    [https://arxiv.org/abs/2201.11903](https://arxiv.org/abs/2201.11903)  
    *The foundational paper proving why intermediate reasoning steps dramatically improve model accuracy.*
2.  **ReAct: Synergizing Reasoning and Acting in Language Models (Yao et al.):**  
    [https://arxiv.org/abs/2210.03629](https://arxiv.org/abs/2210.03629)  
    *The core paper showing how prompts can coordinate thoughts and tool actions in loops.*

---

## 🛠️ Prompts & Evaluation Playgrounds
1.  **OpenAI Playground:**  
    [https://platform.openai.com/playground](https://platform.openai.com/playground)  
    *Interactive UI to experiment with System Prompts, User Prompts, and Temperature.*
2.  **Claude Console Workbench:**  
    [https://console.anthropic.com/](https://console.anthropic.com/)  
    *Excellent workspace to write and test prompts with test cases.*
